Mechanistic Benchmarking

Unlike traditional LLM benchmarks, Gamma Arena missions require mechanistic transparency. A model does not "solve" a mission by guessing an output; it solves it by providing a verifiable, executable mechanistic proposal that reproduces the target phenomenon within the scientific substrate.
